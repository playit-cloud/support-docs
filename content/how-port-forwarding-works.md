## What is port forwarding?

Port forwarding is what allows a computer on the internet to connect to a IP address and then establish a connection with the server on that port. Playit.gg helps with this because it disguises your real IP, gives free DOS protection, and only exposes one port.

## Why can't I port foward on my router?

In most cases you can. There are a few downsides. Your IP address can change randomly (making users have to change it again), you are not protected aginst a DOS attack (this can take down your internet), and this is more insecure as you send users your real IP address.

## How does port forwarding work?

Port forwarding is simple. Imagine it as a security guard at a bar. You must have been requested first before you are let past. Port forwarding opens this "bar" and allows you to recieve connections without you starting them. This is availible on all IP addresses, however your ISP (internet service provider) may block this for security and not let you use it. Port forwarding is also disabled by default in your router. If you are unable to access your router interface, or you are unable to have your ISP unblock it, this is where Playit.gg comes in. Playit.gg uses the normal internet, and passes all traffic over that to a client in your network which does the connections for you.
